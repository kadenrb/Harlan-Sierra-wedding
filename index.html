You're Invited!

We are delighted to invite you to celebrate our wedding at the breathtaking Preacher's Point, located on Abraham Lake, Alberta. Camping Details

We will be arriving a week prior to the wedding to reserve space for everyone. Please note the following important information.

Number of Sites: Sites are not defined in the rustic camping areas.

Amenities: Toilets are available on-site.

Campground Host: There is no campground host.

Reservations: Camping is first-come, first-served. We encourage you to arrive early to secure your spot.

A Public Lands Camping Pass is required for overnight stays at the site. The pass costs $30 per adult, valid for the year.

Alternative Accommodations: For guests who prefer not to camp, the charming town of Nordegg is just 45 minutes away and offers a variety of cozy Airbnbs that are well worth considering for your stay. We look forward to celebrating this unforgettable day with you!
