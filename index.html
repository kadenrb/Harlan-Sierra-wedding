North Dyke Campground

Large, open sites are well suited to RVs and have close access to the water. Here you can hike, mountain bike, swim or just relax by the water. Go sailing, wind surfing, canoeing, boating, or fishing on the reservoir.

South Dyke Campground

South Dyke Campground offers spacious sites that are suitable for RVs and tents and have some shelter from mixed forest surrounding the campground. Here you can hike, mountain bike, swim or relax on the beach. Go sailing, wind surfing, canoeing, boating, or fishing on the large reservoir.


Camping Details


Site Type: Unserviced

Number of Campsites: 14

Power: None
